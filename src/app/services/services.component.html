Our Services


Our services are aimed at ensuring successful software implementations for our clients from start to finish.


Project Management


At ZENTAE Consulting, we have qualified project managers that will ensure successful project delivery from start to finish. Often there are multiple independent parties with their own interests involved with the implementation of large-scale SAP solutions. This is the No. 1 contributor to siloed thinking and non-coherent decisions in projects

Our project managers will manage project deliverables across all delivery channels. We will also ensure that any project design/implementation decisions are made with the project and client’s best interests in mind. ZENTAE Consulting will also evaluate the entire value chain of the new SAP solution to ensure processes are optimized for maximum return on investment.

In summary, our service offerings to you are:

  • Business Case Development
  • Overall Project Management
  • Process Engineering/Re-Engineering
  • Solution Analysis & Optimization

SAP Software Solutions


ZENTAE Consulting is a certified implementation partner for SAP Concur. We offer products in the domains of Travel, Expense and Invoice Management. All products are cloud-based SaaS solutions with the aim to offer enhanced control, process efficiency and cost reduction for business areas which historically have lacked these value-adds.

Travel



SAP Concur Travel is an integrated online travel booking tool that unites all travel information across the enterprise. Data from travelers’ favorite apps, credit cards, and vendors are all integrated into one environment. With all the data in one place, it’s easier to monitor spending, gain insights, ensure compliance, and maintain duty of care.


Expense



With SAP Concur Expense Management, Finance regains control and granular visibility of expenses, reduce fraud and errors, and significantly reduce processing time. SAP Concur Expense utilizes state of the art technology to ensure faster expense submissions and reimbursements. A solution that puts both the employee and the company first.


Invoice



SAP Concur Invoice is a smart Purchase Request/Order and Invoice Management solution. SAP Concur Invoice automates the invoice management process by helping businesses intelligently capture, validate, and pay vendor invoices at a fraction of the time. All of this from one familiar environment. Your SAP Concur environment.

Integration


Integration, whether it is via FTP, API, PaaS or custom-built connectors, is the backbone of any system functioning as expected. Without accurate data, any system looses its value proposition to an organization. At ZENTAE, we strive to make integration easy, functional and cost effective. Our consultants work together with your team to architect a solution that allows your organization to be agile and respond to ever evolving business requirements. Our solutions are fully scalable and will grow alongside your business.

Ask us more about our services:

  • Integration Platform Assessment
  • Cost/Benefit Analysis.
  • Integration Readiness Assessment.
  • Migration Planning & Execution.

Managed Services


Organizations tend to outsource support services for SaaS solutions due to the ever-increasing demand to reduce overheads, the technical expertise required to support SaaS solutions and the basic premise that system support is not core business for most organizations.

Our proven managed services framework is built on the principles of customer satisfaction, data transparency, lean & efficient processes and ensuring best practices. ZENTAE Consulting operates and manages SAP Concur solutions for customers in multiple countries across different industries. Who better to manage and support your SaaS solution than the implementation partner that built it.

We offer managed services for:

  • All SAP Concur Products (Travel, Expense and Invoice)
  • Integration Platform (FTP, API, native or PaaS)
  • SAP Concur BI (IBM Cognos)
  • Solution-Oriented Change Management

Our managed services framework includes:

  • Full integration with your service desk solution based for ticket-handover/ticket-binding.
  • A comprehensive service catalogue.
  • A flexible SLA framework.
  • Up to 24/7 time-zone coverage.

Training & Change Management

Immersive training and change Management ensures that this user base is equipped with the relevant knowledge and insight required to achieve this expectation.

ZENTAE Consulting offers bespoke training and change management initiatives in the SAP solutions domain to ensure a successful go-live for all end-users.

Ask us more about our services:

  • Training and Change Management Strategies
  • Communication Templates (Digital & Print)
  • Classroom Training (End-User, Super-User, Administrative Function)
  • Interactive Digital Training
  • Refresher Training Courses

Business Process Optimization


A software solution can maximize its return on investment if the business processes that are core to the solution are optimized. In order to fully optimize any software solution’s core and supporting processes, the processes firstly need to be understood, documented, analyzed and then finally optimized.

ZENTAE Consulting business analysts use state-of-the-art process documentation tools, leveraging off BPMN (Business Process Model and Notation) principles to document and understand your organization’s processes. Using our years of experience in the relevant business cycles such as Procure to Pay, Travel & Expense Management and Order to Cash, ZENTAE Consulting will optimize these processes to fully support your new SAP software solution.

Ask us more about our services:

  • Business Process Optimization
  • Business Process Automation
  • Business Process Standardization
  • Process Adoption and Change Management
