About Us


ZENTAE is an ICT products and services company with a strong focus and passion for delivering implementations of the SAP Concur product suite.

Our advantage lies in the fact that we were once the client. We are well aware of the need for customer-centric processes to support the deliverables of the product and realization of the targeted ROI.

Founded in 2017, ZENTAE has regional offices in South Africa and Australia. ZENTAE works with clients in the APAC, EMEA, LATAM and NORAM regions and has a successful global footprint stretching across 6 continents, 11 countries and more than 100 successful site implementations.
